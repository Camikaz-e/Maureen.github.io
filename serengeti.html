Package Overview

Embark on a classic safari adventure in the legendary Serengeti National Park. Witness the drama of the Great Migration, where herds thunder across the plains and predators stalk the grasslands. Enjoy game drives through endless savannah, marvel at acacia-dotted landscapes, and experience the magic of sunrise and sunset in the wild.

This 5-day safari includes expertly guided game drives, luxury tented camp or lodge accommodation, and opportunities to see the Big Five, cheetahs, giraffes, and more. From the Seronera Valley’s year-round wildlife to the Mara River’s dramatic crossings, every day brings a new adventure.

  • • Year-round wildlife viewing

Day by Day Itinerary

1

Arrival in Arusha & Transfer to Serengeti

Arrive in Arusha, meet your safari guide, and transfer to Serengeti National Park by road or bush flight. Enjoy your first game drive en route to your luxury camp or lodge. Settle in, soak up the views, and enjoy a welcome dinner under the stars.

Transfer Game Drive Welcome Dinner
2

Central Serengeti & Seronera Valley

Explore the heart of Serengeti, famous for its big cats and abundant wildlife. Enjoy morning and afternoon game drives in the Seronera Valley, spotting lions, leopards, elephants, and herds of wildebeest and zebra. Optional hot air balloon safari at sunrise.

Game Drives Big Cats Balloon Safari
3

Western Corridor & Grumeti River

Journey to the Western Corridor, home to the dramatic Grumeti River crossings during migration. Watch for crocodiles, hippos, and herds braving the waters. Enjoy a picnic lunch in the bush and spot giraffes, buffalo, and a variety of birdlife.

River Crossing Picnic Lunch Bird Watching
4

Northern Serengeti & Mara River

Head north to the Mara River, the stage for the most thrilling migration crossings. Witness herds gathering at the riverbanks, predators lurking, and the raw drama of survival. Evening sundowner with panoramic views of the plains.

Mara River Migration Sundowner
5

Final Game Drive & Departure

Enjoy a final sunrise game drive, capturing last glimpses of the Serengeti’s wildlife. After breakfast, transfer back to Arusha or onward to your next destination, carrying unforgettable memories of Africa’s wild heart.

Game Drive Farewell Departure
